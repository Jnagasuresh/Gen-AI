## Stemming:

Stemming is a process of reducing a word to its root form. It reducing a word to its word stem that affixes to suffixes and prefixes or to the roots of wordss known as lemma. For example, the root form of "running", "ran", and "runs" is "run".

## Lemmatization:

Lemmatization is a process of reducing a word to its root form. For example, the root form of "running", "ran", and "runs" is "run".

## Tokenization:

Tokenization is a process of splitting a text into smaller units called tokens. For example, the tokenization of "running ran and runs" is ["running", "ran", "and", "runs"].

## Stop Words Removal:

Stop words removal is a process of removing common words from a text. For example, the stop words removal of "running ran and runs" is ["running", "ran", "runs"].
